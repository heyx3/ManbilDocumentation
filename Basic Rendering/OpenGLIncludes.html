  • enum PrimitiveTypes;
    • Various types of primitive shapes that can be rendered with shaders.
    • PT_POINTS
      • Individual points, defined by single vertices.
    • PT_LINE_LIST
      • Individual lines, defined by pairs of vertices.
    • PT_LINE_STRIP
      • Connected lines -- each verex defines a line that connects to the previous vertex.
    • PT_TRIANGLE_LIST
      • Individual triangles, defined by groups of 3 vertices.
    • PT_TRIANGLE_STRIP
      • Connected triangles -- each vertex defines a triangle with it and the previous two vertices.

  • enum Shaders
    • The different types of OpenGL shaders.
    • SH_VERTEX
      • Vertex shader
      • Takes in each vertex and outputs its screen-space coordinates.
    • SH_FRAGMENT
      • Fragment shader
      • Takes in the information output by the vertex shader and outputs the color of the geometry's surface.
    • SH_GEOMETRY
      • Geometry shader
      • Optional shader that modifies the primitives being output from the vertex shader.
