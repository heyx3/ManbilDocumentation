Usage

  • This class is a data structure that represents information about a 3D point of view. When taken along with camera position, forward vector, and upward vector, a 3D view fustrum can be calculated.
  • It is encapsulated in the Camera class.
  • Field of view, or FOV, is the vertical angle of the region the 3D perspective can see.
  • Aspect ratio is the ratio of width / height.
  • The horizontal field of view of some projection "info" can be computed with info.FOV * info.GetAspectRatio().
  • zNear and zFar are the closest and farthest distances from the perspective that are visible, respectively.
